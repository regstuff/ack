# The Lost Gram

Once a king and his army had encamped outside a city. A monkey watched them closely from a tree. "I have been feeling hungry all day but now it appears I'll have a feast!" 

A little later, the soldiers brought out big pots. "Let's roast some gram for the horses." 

"Ha! Just as I expected. How lucky I am!" the monkey rejoiced. 

"We'll feed the horses when the gram gets cold," the soldiers said, walking away. 

"This is my chance!" As the soldiers turned to leave, the monkey swooped down from the tree... and ate a handful of gram. "This is delicious!" 

But though his hunger was satisfied, his greed was not. "I must take away as much as I can!" 

He stuffed his mouth with gram... and grabbed some more with both front paws. "I'll have enough for many more days!" he thought, climbing back up the tree. 

But as he climbed, one grain rolled out and... fell on the ground. 

"Oh... Oh, my gram! It's gone!" the monkey gasped. 

Without thinking, he threw away all the gram he had in his paws and... sprang down to look for the one fallen grain. 

"Where could it have gone? It must be somewhere around here!" he searched frantically in the dirt. 

"I can't find it anywhere! And, alas, I have lost the rest of the gram too!" he cried in despair. 

Thus, the foolish monkey, in being niggardly about one grain of gram, lost all he had.
